Name: Alcampo Lounge

Address: London Rd, Brighton BN1 4JF

Review

When you arrive at Alcampo Lounge you will be greeted by a friendly atmosphere and friendly and welcoming staff members. As you look around to find a space for you to sit you will see a noticeably large space with spaced out seating areas so it feels like you have space of your own to choose from. The staff will help find you the right space for you that you feel comfortable and will enjoy your time spent at the venue. Alcampo Lounge meets the needs of dietary requirements such as having a gluten free menu or a vegan menu. Alcampo Lounge has an accessible toilet and is also a dog friendly venue.
